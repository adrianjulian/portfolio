About Me

I am Adrian Carl N. Julian, 21 years old and residing in Sta Ana Manila. I am a third-year college student in one of the most prestigious schools in Manila which is De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde. As of the moment, I work under a Korean company named Cornerstone LTD. My job description includes directing the social media account by publishing poster announcements, observing the algorithms of the accounts, connecting to other brands, reaching different clients, and creating a marketing campaign that helps result in brand awareness. I also do their product photography, promotional videos, and graphic designs.

My specialization would be magazine making, lay-out, branding, logo making, product shoot, photography, videography, and, painting. I am also a versatile person, with my creative juices, I’m able to produce quality output and match the client’s taste. I’ve come to realize that I can be a bridge to the brands who are having a hard time communicating their brand, with my visions in branding and arts, I can translate their ideas into reality with my artistic visions.
